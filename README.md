## Why CleanMyMacbook?

The traditional "macOS Maintenance" market is a monopoly. Companies charge recurring $80/year subscriptions for apps that perform basic system cleanups. That ends today.

CleanMyMacbook is a completely free, highly advanced System Auditing Dashboard. It doesn't just clean caches—it exposes hidden operations running on your machine natively.

## The Features Competitors Won't Build

I didn't stop at emptying the trash. CleanMyMacbook provides genuine intelligence:

- **Active Network Sockets**: See exactly which applications are actively holding network connections open to the internet on your personal computer.
- **Process Verification**: Scans background processes and identifies untrusted background trackers that are consuming your resources silently.
- **Native Startup Daemons**: Exposes the hidden frameworks aggressively forcing themselves to run quietly every time you turn your MacBook on.
- **Leftover Installers**: Locates massive, forgotten installers wasting gigabytes of space on your drive.
- **Raw Administrator Maintenance**: Executes real, deep system maintenance commands that actually force macOS to optimize itself, rather than simulating it.

## Installation

This repository contains the standalone executable application.

1. Download the `CleanMyMacbook.dmg` executable.
2. Open it and drag the `.app` into your Applications folder.
3. *Note: If you receive a security prompt on your first launch, simply right-click the Application and select "Open" manually.*

> **Maximize Power**: Ensure you go to `System Settings > Privacy & Security > Full Disk Access` and explicitly grant CleanMyMacbook permissions to let it natively scan the entire system!
